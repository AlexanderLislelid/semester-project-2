About Us

GAVEL is every student's premier destination for rare and valuable collectibles.

Our mission

We believe that the thrill of the auction should be accessible to everyone. GAVEL gives students a safe, credit-based environment to discover, bid on, and sell unique items without any real-money risk.

How we started

Founded in 2026 as a semester project, GAVEL has grown into a trusted platform used by students worldwide who share a passion for collectibles and fair competition.

Our values

  • Transparency: every bid and listing is visible to all users.
  • Fairness: credits level the playing field for every student.
  • Community: we grow together through shared interests.
