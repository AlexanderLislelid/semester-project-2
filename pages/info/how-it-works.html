How it works

Getting started on GAVEL is simple. Follow these four steps and you'll be bidding and selling in minutes.

1

Create a free account

Sign up in seconds — no credit card required.

2

Get 1000 credits

Every new member receives 1000 credits to start bidding right away.

3

Bid on listings

Browse active listings and place bids. Win the auction to claim the item.

4

Sell your own items

Create a listing, set a deadline, and earn credits when someone wins your auction.
